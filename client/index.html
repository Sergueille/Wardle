game rules

This game is just like New York Times' Wordle, but in multiplayer.

Guess...

You and your opponent have to guess the same secret word. To gain clues, enter an existing 5-letter word each turn, while your opponent does the same. You have 6 turns to guess the word.

... sabotage...

Then, sabotage one letter in your opponent's word. They will not have any information on the letter you choose.

... and get hints.

Finally, you see hints for your word. Each color has a specific meaning:

  • The letter E is in the secret word, but in the wrong spot.
  • The letter L has been sabotaged by your opponent. It may or may not be in the word.
  • The other letters are not in the secret word.
